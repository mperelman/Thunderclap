## Key Patterns

### Cousinhoods (Small Intermarried Elites)
- Banking dominated by **small intermarried elite families**, NOT entire populations
- Example: NOT "Jewish bankers" (too broad) → *Rothschild*, *Warburg* families specifically
- Example: NOT "Puritan bankers" (too broad) → Boston Brahmin (Cabot, Lowell) specifically

### Kinlinks Across Cousinhoods (FUNDAMENTAL - The Norm, Not Exception)
- **Most cousinhoods kinlinked extensively outside their grouping**
- Banking networks crossed religious/ethnic lines through marriage and partnerships
- Cross-group kinlinks were **essential** to banking access and power, not occasional

#### Major Cross-Group Networks:

**London Cousinhood** (Multi-religious):
- *Schroder* (Germanic Protestant)
- *Baring* (Germanic Protestant)
- Smyth (Anglican)
- *Hambro* (converted Jewish)
- Cobbold
- All heavily kinlinked despite different origins

**Protestant Cologne + Jewish Kinlinks:**
- *Stein*, *Schaaffhausen* (Protestant Cologne)
- Kinlinked with *Oppenheim* (Jewish Court Jews)

**Puritan + Boston Brahmin:**
- *Morgan* (Puritan)
- *Lee Higginson* (Puritan)
- Kinlinked with Cabot, Lowell, Forbes (Boston Brahmin elite)

**Greek + Huguenot:**
- Greek Orthodox families married Huguenots

**Sephardim + Ashkenazim:**
- Initially separate diasporas (Sephardim: Amsterdam/Ottoman; Ashkenazim: Poland/Germany)
- Later kinlinked with each other

These networks show that **identity shaped access**, but **kinlinks transcended identity** (and even subgroups) to create powerful banking coalitions.

### Minority Middlemen Pattern
- Common across groups: Exclusion from land/politics/industry → channeled into finance
- Jewish: Excluded from land ownership, guilds
- Quaker: Excluded from universities, Test Acts barred from office
- Huguenot: Refugees fleeing France, excluded in Catholic countries
- Parsee: Zoroastrian minority in Mughal/British India
- Armenian: Christian minority in Ottoman Empire

## Name Changes (Automatically Detected & Linked)

The system automatically detects and links name changes from the documents:

- **Warburg** ← DelBanco (Sephardim origin)
- **Brodsky** ← Schorr
- **Goldsmid/Goldet** ← Goldschmidt

### How It Works:
- Search "Warburg" → Also searches "DelBanco" automatically
- Search "DelBanco" → Also searches "Warburg" automatically
- Bidirectional linking ensures comprehensive results

- Anglicization (DelBanco → Warburg)
- Geographic migration
- Assimilation strategies
- Marriage conventions
